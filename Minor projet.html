Historical Places in India

Red Fort

Located in Delhi, the Red Fort is a symbol of Mughal power and one of the most famous monuments in India.

Taj Mahal

Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, it's one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

Qutub Minar

Located in Delhi, the Qutub Minar is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the tallest brick minaret in the world.

Gateway of India

Located in Mumbai, the Gateway of India was built during the British Raj and commemorates the visit of King George V and Queen Mary to India in 1911.

Jaipur Palace

The City Palace in Jaipur is a magnificent blend of Rajasthani and Mughal architecture and a testament to royal splendor.
